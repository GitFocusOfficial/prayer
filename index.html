Prayer Before Exams

Dear Jesus, today I will have my examinations, you know how important they are to me, so I am humbly asking Your gracious help and divine assistance. I pray to You, my dear Jesus, please never let me panic nor get nervous, just let me be ease and give my very best. Please never let me guess nor rely on pure luck, but enlighten my mind and let me think dearly. Please never let me resort to chances nor to dishonestly, but let me work to the fullest of my ability. I pray for Your guidance that as I think, I may find the right solutions. I may be able to correctly answer the questions, I may solve those difficult problems. Let me, with your help, give my best effort. Amen.

Rosary to the Sacred Wounds of Our Lord Jesus Christ

3 Small Beads
      O' Jesus Divine Redeemer, Have Mercy On Us and the Whole World.
      Forgive Mercy My Jesus during danger cover us with your most previous blood.
      Eternal Father, have mercy for the sake of the blood of our Lord Jesus Christ Your Only Son, have mercy on us we beg of thee, Amen, Amen, Amen.

Big Bead

Eternal Father, I offer you the wounds of Our Lord Jesus Christ to Heal the Wounds of Our Soul.

Ten Small Beads

My Jesus pardon and mercy for the sake of your wounds.

Prayer to the Sacred Wounds

O most loving Jesus, meek, lamb of God, I, a miserable sinner salute and worship the most sacred wounds on thy shoulder on which thou didst bear thy heavy cross which so tore thy flesh and laid bare thy bones as to inflict on thee an anguish greater than any other wounds on thy blessed body. I adore thee Oh Jesus most sorrowful. I praise and glorify thee. I give thee thanks for these most sacred and painful wounds. Beseeching thee by the exceeding pain to be merciful to me a sinner. To forgive me all my mortal and venial sins, and to lead me on towards heaven along the way of thy cross amen.

Prayers to the Holy Spirit

Holy Spirit thou who make me see everything and show me the way to reach my ideal, you who give me the divine gift to forgive and forget the wrong that is done to me and who are in all instances in my life with me. I, in this short dialogue, want to thank you for everything and confirm once more that I never want to be separated from you no matter how great the material desires may be. I want to be with you and my loved ones in your perpetual glory, amen. Note: A person must pray this 3 consecutive days without stating one's wish. After the 3rd day your wish will be granted no matter how difficult it may be. Promise to share this prayer as soon as your favor has been granted.
